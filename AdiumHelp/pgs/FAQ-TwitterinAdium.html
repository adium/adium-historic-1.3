Twitter in Adium


Will Adium support Twitter?

Yes. Adium 1.4 and later will let you connect to Twitter accounts.

The old way

Twitter used to have a feature that let you use it from any Jabber-compatible instant-messaging service, including Jabber itself, Google Talk, LiveJournal, and the Gizmo Project. As you may know, all Jabber-compatible services are among the many services that Adium supports.

Unfortunately, Twitter turned off that feature back in May 2008. We're still waiting for them to turn it back on.

You can still read the instructions to enable instant-messaging for your Twitter account, but if you try them now, you'll notice that you can't get past step 2.

The Twitter support in 1.4 will provide the same character-counter.
