iTunes integration

Adium includes the ability to specify the current song playing in iTunes.

Tokens

In versions of Adium before 1.0, you could use the shortcut /music to display the track in the form "Track - Artist". This can now be accomplished with the token %_iTunes.

Below is a list of the tokens available in Adium 1.0 for use in statuses, profiles or text chat.

Token name Text replacement
%_iTunes A pre-formatted entry in the form: %_track - %_artist
%_music A pre-formatted entry in the form: /me is listening to %_track by %_artist
%_status Specifies the status if iTunes (i.e., Playing or Paused)
%_artist The artist of the current song.
%_album The album of the current song.
%_genre The genre of the current song.
%_track The track name of the current song.
%%_composer The track name of the current song.
%_iTMS A link to the iTunes Music Store for the current song.

Status

To simply set your available status to the current song playing, choose the builtin "â™« iTunes" status from the status dropdown list.

If you want to show the currently playing song in iTunes in your custom Available and Away statuses, first edit your status and then select your choice of iTunes script from the "Insert Script" submenu of the "Edit" menu, or by specifying one of the above tokens.


Profiles

You can also add the current playing song in iTunes in your AOL Instant Messenger or MobileMe (formerly known as .Mac) profile by editing your profile and then selecting your choice of iTunes script from the "Insert Script" submenu of the "Edit" menu.
